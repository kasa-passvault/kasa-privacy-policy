KASA Password Manager – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

KASA Password Manager is built with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains what data KASA processes, how it works, and how your information is handled.

1. Data Storage

KASA stores your vault data locally on your device. Passwords, notes, attachments, and related data are encrypted and remain under your control. KASA does not upload your vault to any external server.

2. No Account Required

KASA does not require account registration. You can use the app without creating an online account.

3. No Cloud Sync

KASA does not provide cloud synchronization. Your encrypted data stays on your device unless you manually export a backup.

4. Backups

If you choose to create a backup, the backup file is encrypted before export. Backup files are only stored in the location selected by you. KASA does not receive or store copies of your backups.

5. Internet Access

KASA is designed to work primarily offline. Sensitive vault content is not transmitted to remote servers.

6. Biometric Authentication

If enabled, biometric authentication uses your device’s secure biometric system. Biometric data is processed by your device and is never accessible to KASA.

7. Data Sharing

KASA does not sell, collect, or share personal data with third parties.

8. User Control

You remain the owner of your data at all times. You may edit, export, delete, or permanently remove your vault data whenever you choose.
